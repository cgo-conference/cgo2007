CGO 2007 Local Information

Venue

CGO 2007 will be held at Hotel Valencia Santana Row in San Jose, CA.

Arrival information

Click here for driving directions to the hotel. If you are not from the Bay Area, you will probably arrive to one of the three local airports. The San Jose airport is the closest one. The other options are the San Franciso airport and the Oakland airport.

If you are not renting a car, you can either use a shared van from the airport or get to the hotel by public transit. You can find out the public transit info from the 511 Trip Planner web site. Enter, the airport name as the orgin ("SFO, San Francisco", "OAK, Oakland" or "SJC, San Jose") and "Santana Row, San Jose" as the destination.

If you are driving from the Bay Area and are not familiar with Santana Row, click here for a map of Santana Row that shows parking.

Things to do

  • The hotel is located in Santana Row with many restaurants and stores.
  • On the other side of Stevens Creek Blvd, you can find more restaurants in the Valley Fair shopping mall.
  • Winchester Mystery House (walking distance from the hotel).
  • The Tech Museum of Innovation
  • The Rosicrucian Egyptian Museum
  • San Jose Museum of Art
