Visa information for foreign participants of CGO-5

International registrants should be particularly aware and careful about visa requirements, and should plan travel well in advance. Please note that visa letters will only be issued to confirm participation for:

  • Speakers/Presenters
  • Committee Members
  • Attendees who have paid their registration fee in full
who are not from one of the countries embargoed by the U.S. Treasury Department's Office of Foreign Assets Control.
