CGO 2007 Student and Junior Faculty Travel Support

A limited number of junior faculty and student travel grants are available to those attending CGO. Grants will be no less than $200, but no more than actual expenses. Applications will be accepted up to the Tuesday, March 13, 2007. Applicants that are early registered will receive preference. Actual payment will occur after the close of the conference. Preference will be given to conference presenters, then workshop presenters, then attendees.

To apply for a grant please send an email to Teresa Johnson (teresa.johnson AT hp.com) with the subject line "CGO Travel Grant". The following information in the message body:

  • Your name.
  • Your affiliation.
  • Whether you are a student or a junior faculty member.
  • Whether you are a co-author of a paper in the main symposium or one of the workshops. (Please give the name of the paper as well.)
  • Your estimated costs.
